10. Starting from Top Left and proceeding in the left to right order

Top left window: This window displays the entire tree structure of the 
project. It shows the branches, merges and all other such data.

Top Middle window: This displays a list of the authors who have made 
	commits.

Top Right Window: Displays the dates on which the commits were made.

Bottom Left Window: Displays the Log details for the commit that we are 
looking at.

Bottom Right Window: Contains a list of the modified files in the commit
